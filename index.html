WHO WE ARE

We love to serve the Lord through the word.

The church I dream of is where all people are cared for, known, equipped and unleashed to live out the Gospel. We will invest all we have to that end. Fellowship is a group of people from all races and classes. We look differently, live differently and vote differently from each other, yet the Gospel penetrates these differences. It changes everything about the way we live, the way we are, and how we see the world. As individuals, we intentionally choose to lay aside our differences to have communion with each other and with God. As a community, we strive to live out the Gospel. “When I dream of the church of Jesus Christ, I think of Revelation 7:9, where people from every nation, tribe, people and language stand before the throne and declare ‘Worthy is the Lamb of God.’ In the book of Ephesians, the Apostle Paul describes the church as God’s greatest glory. If we are going to transform the world, we are going to do it through the church.

Pst. George Muchiri, Lead Pastor

We believe Jesus’ Word is relevant, powerful, and life-changing. We also believe there is a famine for true Biblical preaching in the Church throughout America. We have determined to boldly proclaim His Word and to celebrate life together as the Church to the glory of the One who’s name is above all names! We invite you to experience life with us at Fellowship. Come as you are and join us as we pursue life together as the Church and the Community of Believers. Come join us and find out what living is all about.
